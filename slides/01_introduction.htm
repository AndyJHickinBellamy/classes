Standards, Quirks, XHTML

  • Doctype sets the "mode" (why)
  • Void elements need no trailing slash
  • Case and quotes don't matter
  • Attribute values not required (can be on and off)
  • Link relations clarified (here)

New: <header>

  • Groups introductory or navigation aids
  • Can appear multiple times
<header>
	<img src="logo.png" alt="Welcome to HTMLVille"/>
	<h1>We are HTMLVille</h1>
</header>

New: <section>

  • A thematic grouping of content
  • Chapters, tabbed pages, news areas
<section>
	<p>
	    In this section we will ... 
	</p>
</section>

New: <article>

  • A self-contained composition
  • Independently distributable
<article>
	<h1>
	    Mating Habits of the Wolly Mammoth
	</h1>
	...
</article>

New: <nav>

  • Major navigation blocks
  • Contains links to other pages
  • Improves accessibility
<nav>
	<a href="/home">Home</a>
	<a href="/products">Products</a>
	<a href="/complaints">Complaints</a>
</nav>

New: <aside>

  • Tangentially related content
  • Sidebar, list of links
<aside>
	<p>Related</p>
	<a href="http://html5doctor.com/understanding-aside/">
	    Aside Revisited
	</a>
</aside>

New: <footer>

  • Footer for the nearest ancestor
  • Can have multiple footers
<footer>
	(c) 2011 OdeToCode LLC
</footer>

Other new elements

  • <hgroup> - grouping of header elements
  • <time> - pubdate and datetime
  • <mark> - highlighted text
<article>
  <header>
    <hgroup>
      <h1>Title</h1>
      <h2>Subtitle</h2>
    </hgroup>
    <p>
      <time datetime="2010-03-20">11th June, 2011</time>
    </p>	
  </header>                
  <p>
    This might be my <mark>birthday</mark>
  </p>
</article>

Meter & Progress

  • <progress> - Completion amount
  • <meter> - Score or result
A+ 
<div>
	<progress value="25" max="100"></progress>
	<meter value="91" min="0" max="100" low="40" 
	       high="90" optimum="100">A+</meter>
</div>

Figures

  • <figure> - unit of content with optional caption
  • <figcaption> - the caption or legend for a figure
<figure>
   <code>
       $(function() { ... });
   </code>
   <figcaption>jQuery in action</figcaption>
</figure>

Details and Summaries

  • <details> - a disclosure widget
  • <summary> - the caption or legend for a figure
More links
  • Link 1
  • Link 2
  • Link 3
<details>
  <summary>More links</summary>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="">Link 1</a></li>
      <li><a href="">Link 2</a></li>
      <li><a href="">Link 3</a></li>
    </ul>
</details>
